Who We Are

SPM Engineering Works was established in 2022 and is located in Hosur, Tamil Nadu. The company specializes in welding, chassis fabrication, and engineering manufacturing solutions for the automobile industry.

With experienced leadership and skilled technicians, the company focuses on delivering high-quality products, precision manufacturing, and timely delivery for its customers.

Vision & Mission

To serve customers globally by delivering reliable engineering solutions and maintaining long-term partnerships.

Our Mission

Deliver economic, ethical, and excellent quality manufacturing while ensuring complete customer satisfaction.
